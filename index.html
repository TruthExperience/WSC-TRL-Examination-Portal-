Passing Standards
Full Pass — 95%+
Certification granted immediately. No further review required. Applies to all exams at the full threshold.
Provisional — 90–94%
WSC Driver exams only. Conditional licence issued, subject to staff review. All other exams: this score is a fail.
Fail — Below Threshold
Certification denied. Full retake required. No partial credit. No exceptions. Merit cannot be rounded up.
How It Works
01
AI Generated
Every exam generates a completely fresh set of questions from the official rulebook on each attempt. No two exams are identical.
02
Timed & Strict
Server-enforced countdown timer. Exam auto-submits at expiry. Answers given so far are scored. No pausing.
03
Three Difficulty Tiers
Questions calibrated across straightforward rule recall, applied scenario, and hard edge cases.
04
Full Review
After submission, full explanations are revealed for every question. Retake with a completely fresh exam immediately.
