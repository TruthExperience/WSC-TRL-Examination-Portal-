Passing Standards

Full Pass — 95%+
Certification granted immediately. No further review required.
Provisional — 90–94%
Conditional licence issued, subject to staff review. WSC Driver exams only.
Fail — Below Threshold
Licence denied. Full retake required. No partial credit. On TRL exams, 94% is an Institutional Failure. Merit cannot be rounded up.

Special Rules

★ WSC Stewards scoring 98%+ receive an optional invitation to sit the TRL Stewards Certification exam.
★ TRL F1 Driver exam is Tier and Era gated. Select Elite / Apex Pro / Apex / Academy and endorsement [E-GE] / [E-AP] / [E-NC] at the start.
★ Practical scenarios require ruling on a live incident clip under your league's rulebook. Both components must pass for steward certification.
★ WSC exam pass results at 95%+ are automatically reported to WSC Staff.

How It Works

01 Select your exam. Questions are generated fresh from the official rulebooks on every attempt. No two exams are identical.
02 Timer starts immediately and is server-side. It cannot be paused, bypassed, or reset by clearing cache.
03 Submit before time expires or the exam auto-submits with answers given so far. Full explanations are revealed after submission.
04 Failed attempts trigger a mandatory 24-hour lockout before a retake is permitted.
