Special Conditions
WSC → TRL Pathway: WSC Stewards who score 95%+ on the Stewards exam earn an optional invitation to sit the TRL Stewards Certification exam. This is never mandatory.
WSC Results Notification: All passing WSC Steward results are automatically reported to the WSC Admin (xjanx07x). Scores of 95%+ trigger a separate TRL Steward invitation notification.
TRL Era Endorsements: F1 and F2 Driver exams require selecting both a tier AND an era endorsement ([E-GE]/[E-AP]/[E-NC]). Both components are tested in the same exam session.
Proxy Certification: Utilising any proxy to sit a PitBoss exam is a Lifetime Ban offense under TRL Articles of Expulsion v3.0, Article 2.06. The proxy sitter also receives a Lifetime Ban.
Anti-Bypass: All timers are server-side. Clearing cache, closing the tab, or refreshing does not reset the lockout timer or extend exam time.

PitBoss exam questions draw directly from the global FIA regulatory library. The AI question generator has deep knowledge of FIA ISC 2019–2026, all Appendices (A through S), F1/F2/F3 Sporting Regulations, and FIA Appendix E (Esports International Sporting Code, effective January 2025). TRL's governance framework predates and mirrors what the FIA has now formally codified in Appendix E. PitBoss isn't behind the curve — it's ahead of it.
FIA International Sporting Code (ISC) 2019–2026
All 20 core articles. Competitor responsibilities, protests & appeals, sanctions framework, judicial procedures. Foundation of TRL governance.
Appendix E — Esports ISC (Dec 2024)
First esports-specific FIA regulatory framework. International Esports Licence, ASN authority, virtual motorsport definitions. TRL exam system aligned with this standard.
Appendix L — Driver Conduct 2019–2026
International driver licences, medical examinations, driver equipment and conduct standards. Now includes International Esports Licence provisions.
F1 Sporting Regulations 2022–2026
Version delta analysis across game eras: 2022→F1 23, 2023→F1 24, 2024/25→F1 25, 2026→F1 26 DLC. Feeds era endorsement question banks.
FIA F2 Sporting Regulations 2025–2026
Sprint Race & Feature Race structure, driver conduct standards, steward procedures. Foundation for F2 Academy exam questions.
FIA Judicial & Disciplinary Rules 2025
FIA's own disciplinary process — directly relevant to TRL's BSAC and judicial framework. Right of Review vs Right of Appeal distinction.
