## Passing Standards

**Full Pass (95%+)**
Licence or certification granted immediately. No further review required.

**Provisional Pass (90–94%)**
Conditional licence issued — subject to staff review. Applies to WSC Driver exams only.

**Fail (below threshold)**
Licence denied. Full retake required. No partial credit. No exceptions.

---

## How It Works

Every exam is powered by Claude AI and generates a completely fresh set of questions on each attempt. No two exams are identical. Questions are drawn directly from the official WSC and TRL rulebooks and calibrated across three difficulty levels — straightforward, moderate, and hard edge cases.

During the exam there are no hints, no feedback, and no correct answer indicators. Full explanations for every question are revealed only after submission.

---

## Special Notes

- WSC Stewards who score **95% or above** on the Stewards Certification exam earn an optional invitation to sit the TRL Stewards Certification exam
- WSC Driver exam results at **95%+** are automatically reported to WSC Staff
- All exams require an active internet connection to generate questions
- Exams are best taken on desktop or tablet for the full experience

## Rulebook Coverage

**WSC Driver Exams** cover the WSC Unified Sporting & Racing Code v1.2, WSC Steward Operational Manual v1.2, and WSC Draft Proposal operational standards — including the PP licence system, Axle Rule, Calculated Divebomb standard, track limits, zone modifiers, evidence standards, reporting deadlines, and steward workflow.

**WSC Stewards Exam** covers all WSC Driver content plus IRL FIA F2 2025 Sporting Regulations and FIA International Sporting Code principles.

**TRL Stewards Exam** covers the TRL Stewards Charter v3.0, TRL Universal Sporting Code v4.0, PP Calculator, Calculated Divebomb adjudication framework, TRL Articles of Expulsion v3.0, the Unified Probation Framework, and TRL Charter v9.0.
